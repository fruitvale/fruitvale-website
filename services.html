Our Services

Our glaucoma-certified optometrists provide diagnosis and treatment of ocular disease and injury, complete contact lens services including all specialty lens products, co-management for laser and cataract surgeries, and frame styling to complement your lifestyle. Dr. Irani and Dr. Luu are both certified for fitting hybrid SynergEyes and Paragon CRT Ortho-K contact lenses. Outside prescriptions are welcome. Our on-site optical laboratory enables us to provide a fast turnaround on your glasses.

Facilities & equipment

  • In-office optical laboratory
  • Retinal photography
  • Visual field analyzer
  • Autorefractor
  • Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT)
  • Corneal topography

Frames & lenses

We carry budget frames through top designer names — Gucci, Versace, Ray-Ban, Oakley, Prada, Coach and more — plus a full range of lens options and kids' frames. See our frames & lenses page for the complete list.

Insurance & payment

We accept Medi-Cal and most major vision and medical plans, and offer flexible ways to pay including CareCredit. See insurance & payment for the full list of plans we take and what to bring.

Languages spoken
