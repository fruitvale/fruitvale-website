Saturday & Sunday eye exams in Oakland

Need an eye exam but can't take time off during the week? We're open 7 days a week, including Saturday and Sunday, right next to the Fruitvale BART station in Oakland.

Weekend hours

  • Saturday: 10:00 am – 3:00 pm
  • Sunday: 10:00 am – 3:00 pm
  • Monday–Friday: 9:00 am – 6:00 pm

Book online or call (510) 533-6567. Walk-ins are welcome when we have room, but booking ahead means less waiting.

Why weekends work

Weekends are ideal for working families and anyone who can't get away on a weekday — no time off needed, and it's easy to bring the kids for their exams at the same time. We accept Medi-Cal and most major plans, and our on-site lab means many glasses are ready fast.

Easy to reach — next to Fruitvale BART

We're at 3301 East 12th Street #109 in the Fruitvale Village plaza, steps from the Fruitvale BART station, with free weekend parking in the plaza garage. See directions & parking.

A quick note on insurance: many insurance offices are closed on weekends, so if you'd like us to verify your coverage before a Saturday or Sunday visit, call ahead and we'll check it in advance.
