Insurance & Payment

✓ We accept Medi-Cal

We work with Medi-Cal and most major vision and medical plans. Not sure if we take yours? Call (510) 533-6567 and we'll check for you — in English or Spanish.

Have Medi-Cal? See exactly what Medi-Cal covers for eye exams & glasses, or ask about weekend (Sat & Sun) eye exams.

Plans we accept

  • Medi-Cal
  • Alameda Alliance
  • Medicaid
  • Medicare
  • VSP
  • EyeMed
  • Blue Cross / Blue Shield

Don't see your plan? Call us — we may still be able to help, and we can check your coverage before you come in.

No insurance? No problem.

We keep care affordable for everyone. Pay with cash, any major card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or CareCredit (financing for eye care and eyewear). You can also buy an individual vision plan through VSP and use it the same day.

Ways to pay

Cash, debit card, Mastercard, Visa, American Express, contactless payments (Apple Pay and Google Pay), and CareCredit.

What to bring

  • Your insurance or member card (Medi-Cal BIC, plan card, or vision-plan ID)
  • A photo ID
  • Your current glasses or contact lens boxes, if you have them
