Medi-Cal eye exams & glasses in Oakland

✓ We accept Medi-Cal

Fruitvale Optometry is a Medi-Cal provider right next to the Fruitvale BART station, open 7 days a week. If you have Medi-Cal (including plans through Alameda Alliance for Health), you may be covered for a complete eye exam and a pair of glasses — with no copay. Here's how it works.

What Medi-Cal covers for your eyes

  • Eye exams. A routine, complete eye exam is a covered Medi-Cal benefit.
  • Glasses. Eyeglasses are a covered benefit — California restored adult eyeglass coverage in 2020, and children have always been covered.
  • Children & youth (under 21). Full vision coverage, including exams and glasses, under Medi-Cal's children's benefits.
  • Medical eye care. Care for conditions like glaucoma, diabetic eye disease, and eye injuries is also covered.

Exact benefits and frame/lens options depend on your specific plan and eligibility. Call (510) 533-6567 and we'll confirm your coverage before you come in — in English or Spanish.

How often can I get an exam and new glasses?

Adults on Medi-Cal generally qualify for a new eye exam and glasses about every 24 months; children may qualify more often. We check your current eligibility when you book, so you'll know before your visit.

What to bring

  • Your Medi-Cal Benefits Identification Card (BIC) or plan card (Alameda Alliance, etc.)
  • A photo ID
  • Your current glasses or contact-lens boxes, if you have them

Have Alameda Alliance?

Alameda Alliance for Health is a managed Medi-Cal plan we work with. Bring your Alliance card and we'll verify your vision benefits the same way — just give us a call or start your booking below.
