Walk-ins & appointments

It is always best to make an appointment to see the doctors. We try to accommodate walk-ins when possible, but cannot guarantee it. If you'd like to walk in, please call first to check the doctor's schedule for that day — this helps you avoid long wait times.

Usually, Dr. Irani is here Mon, Tue, and Sat; Dr. Hernandez on Wed and Thu; and Dr. Luu on Mon, Fri, and Sun.

Parking

  • Easiest: BART or the bus — 12 lines stop at Fruitvale Village.
  • BART lot: free on weekends and after 3 pm every day.
  • Fruitvale Village garage: first 15 minutes free on weekdays, then $1.00 per 15 minutes.
  • The open lot on East 12th and 35th Ave has closed, so parking is more limited than before. Parking policies can change without notice.
