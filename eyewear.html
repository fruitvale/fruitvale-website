Lens options

Depending on your prescription and lens choice, we finish many jobs right here in our on-site optical lab and send others to our trusted outside labs. Common options include:

Lens designs

  • Single vision (distance or reading)
  • Bifocal and trifocal
  • Progressive "no-line" multifocal

Materials — thinner, lighter, safer

  • Standard plastic (CR-39)
  • Polycarbonate — impact-resistant, great for kids and active wear
  • High-index — thinner and lighter for stronger prescriptions

Coatings & treatments

  • Anti-reflective (AR) — cuts glare and reflections for sharper night vision
  • Scratch-resistant coating
  • UV protection
  • Blue-light filtering — for screens and digital eye strain

Light-adaptive & sun lenses

  • Transitions (photochromic) — clear indoors, darken automatically in sunlight
  • Polarized — cuts glare off roads, water, and snow
  • Tints and prescription sunglasses
