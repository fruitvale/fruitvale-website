Insurance we accept

Not sure what your plan covers? Call (510) 533-6567 and we'll check for you.

  • Medi-Cal
  • Medicaid
  • Medicare
  • VSP
  • Alameda Alliance
  • Blue Cross / Blue Shield
  • EyeMed

What to bring: your insurance card or member ID and a photo ID.

No insurance? We take cash, all major cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and CareCredit. You can also buy a vision plan through VSP.

Services

👁️

Eye exams

Full vision and eye health check, with retinal photos and OCT scans when needed.

👧

Kids' eye exams

Comfortable exams for children, with results explained to parents in English or Spanish.

👓

Glasses

On-site lab for fast turnaround. Budget frames up to Ray-Ban, Gucci, Versace, Oakley, and Nike.

🔍

Contact lenses

Including SynergEyes and Ortho-K specialty lenses. Outside prescriptions welcome.

🩺

Glaucoma & medical eye care

All our doctors are certified to treat glaucoma. We also manage diabetic eye disease, infections, and injuries.

🏥

Surgery co-management

Care before and after cataract or LASIK surgery.
